A first blog post

Hi there. This is my first blog post. Actually, I’ve no clue what to achieve and where to end up with this blog. I’m doing just for my self. Kind of a diary. To document where this journey called life leads me to. To be honest, I’m not even really sure about what the content is going to be about. I think I’m going to write down what ever keeps me busy. I’d like to keep kind of track of whatever I’m learning new. That’s all what it is about. Constantly learning new stuff.

There are several topics I’m currently focused on. First of all, soon, I’m going to be a traveller again. I just bought an RV and I intend to live in it from now on. I don’t know for how long. Let’s figure it out. Another topic I probably cover is all the programming stuff I’m doing for work and in my spare time (JS, Python, Web… ec.). Music, what would I be without my bass guitar? Hmm… yes, I know, I defenitely could practice more… And then I just started learning italian two months ago… oh and not to forget, I still have to improve my english alot.

All right. Let’s dive into it
