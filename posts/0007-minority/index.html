Minority

March 8, 2021 One-minute read
thoughts
vote

When I saw the headlines today, it made me feel very sad and disappointed. Switzerland voted yesterday. We have made it illegal to wear a Burka. There are now dress codes in our law. How weird? I never ever saw a women wearing one in Switzerland. So it’s not about preventing people covering their faces. It’s about discriminating a religion. It’s about stimulating fear of foreign cultures.
Further we declined the launch of an electronic Identity Document. It’s a pity. I hoped we were more open to technological progress.
And the third initiative was about a Free Trade Agreement with Indonesia. It was accepted. I thought we could do without cheap palm oil and not contribute to accelerating the destruction of forests.

In every point I voted for the oposite. Seems like I’m part of the minority in this country. To me, all this looks like a lot of fear. Fear of the unfamiliar. Fear of the digitalization. And the fear of losing wealth. Poor fearful people. You don’t see what opportunities you’re missing.

Be curious. Ask questions. Never stop learning new things. Love life.
